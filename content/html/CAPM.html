CAPM Video Course description

Introduction

CAPM (Certified Associate in Project Management) is one of the popular certifications on project management, regardless on which stage you are in with your career, this video course will help you to build credibility and effectiveness with the project team. Moreover, this video course will give you a great distinction in the job market.

Description

CAPM video course is an excellent medium to develop your role as a project manager. There are plenty of exercises, concise and videos. This will have 25 hours of studies regarding project management taught by an expert which will help you to clear CAPM exam.

The video course covers in detail all the objectives for the PMBOK Guide, Fifth edition. Here is the list of some of the features of this course:

  • Discussion forum with Instructor and peers
  • All the exams are in PDF format for effectiveness
  • 14 module exams covering each section of PMBOK guide
  • Complete and detailed coverage of CAPM concepts

This course will have 17.5 hours of on-demand video, having 30 article, 23 supplement materials and a certificate of completion.

Knowledge and skills students develop:

  • You will get great resources to pass CAPM exam.
  • You will gain knowledge on project management processes.
  • You will be able to demonstrate the theories, formulas, and charts of project management.
  • You will be able to Figure float for system projects and can draw network diagrams.
  • You will be accustomed to core concepts of PMBOK guide
  • After this course, you will be able to apply the formulas and tips & tricks for earned value management.

Requirements for this course:

  • The candidate must know the fundamental of project management.
  • The student should be dedicated to passing CAPM exam.

Audience to target:

  • A project manager who wants to pass CAPM exam.
  • One who wants to clear the exam at the first attempt.
  • The course is not intended for new Project managers.

Exam paper design:

  • CAPM exam will consist of 150 multiple choice questions.
  • The allotted time for the exam is 3 hours.
  • Out of 150 questions, there will be 15 pretest questions and 135 scored questions.
  • The exam is based on PMBOK guide, fifth edition.

Conclusion:

CAPM certification provides recognition to the candidates who have experience in project management. This certification is as per the guidelines of PMBOK that demonstrate the knowledge and proficiency of project management. This certification will help to uplift the project management skills to increase proficiency within the team.
