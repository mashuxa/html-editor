Exam paper design:

  • There will be 120 questions in PMI-ACP certification exam.
  • There will be around 20 pretest questions.
  • There will be 3 hours allotted for the exam.
  • The exam is computer-based.

The question distribution of the exam is as below

 

  • Value-driven Delivery (20% of questions)
  • Stakeholder Engagement (17% of questions)
  • Team Performance (16% of questions)
  • Adaptive Planning (12% o questions)
  • Problem Resolution and Detection (10% of questions)
  • Continuous Improvement (Product, Process, People) (9% of questions)
  • Agile Mindset and Principles (16% of questions)
