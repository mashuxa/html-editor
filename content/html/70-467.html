Microsoft 70-467 Video Course Description

Introduction

Business intelligence is an integral part of SQL Server and 70-467 is the certification designed for the associates who are responsible for the business intelligence design and solutions. Having a business intelligence certification is great values in IT as major platforms are designed with BI. The BI not only helps in simplification of solutions but also helps in implementing it effectively

This video course will provide all the knowledge and skills that are required to clear this exam.

Description

70-467 Designing Business Intelligence solutions video course is intended for the BI architects who are familiar with overall design and decisions around SQL Server 2012 Business intelligence solutions. The video course will judge the below skills of the professional:

  • BI Solution design and architecture
  • Provisioning of hardware and software
  • Data mart design and warehouse
  • Online Analytical Processing (OLAP) cube designing
  • Integration with line-of-business applications or Microsoft SharePoint
  • Windows Azure SQL Database environment designing
  •  

On completion of this video course, you would be MCSE in Business Intelligence certification.

On competition of the course student will develop the following skills

  • Performance and Scalability planning
  • Upgrade management and planning
  • Schema design
  • Cube architecture and design
  • Fact tables design
  • MDX calculations creation and design
  • SSIS package execution ad design
  • Deploying SSIS solutions and design
  • Package configurations design for SSIS package
  • Server health management
  • Security design strategy
  • SQL partitioning strategies
  • Backup strategies
  • Logging and auditing strategies
  • Designing report dataset
  • Excel Services/Reporting management for SharePoint
  • Data acquisition strategies
  • Managing and planning reporting services and configuration
  • BI reporting solution design and architecture
  • Data warehouse solutions
  •  

This course has detailed explanation on the above points.

Requirements for this course:

Knowledge of the basics of SQL Server 2012 is the requirement of this video course. This will cover everything which is needed to pass 70-467 exam. IT Professionals who work on the Business Intelligence of SQL Server platform will be highly benefitted with this course and certification.

The course comes with 4.5 hours on-demand video, full lifetime free access and a certification on completion.

Audience to target:

  • IT Professionals who have the basic knowledge in SQL Server 2012
  • Candidates who want to expertise their knowledge in SQL Server 2012 Business Intelligence

Exam paper design:

  • There will be around 45-55 questions approximately.
  • The test format will be multiple choice and multiple answer questions.
  • The total time for the exam is 120 minutes.
  • Passing score of the exam is 700. The 700 does not imply 70% but based upon Microsoft’s calculation based upon the questions. There is no negative marking for incorrect answers and there will be questions for Microsoft survey which will have no impact on the marking.

This Microsoft 70-467 video course is intended for BI architects and professionals. This will help professionals in building strong knowledge in overall BI solutions.
