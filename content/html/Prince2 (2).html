Introduction

PRINCE2 (Projects IN Controlled Environments) is a famous practitioner certification program that primarily focuses on splitting the projects into controllable stages. It is one of the best methodologies in the world and acquiring the certification will confirm your expertise in Project management which will be a major boost to your professional career.

This training video course will help you clear all concept of PRINCE2 that is needed to pass the exam. You don’t necessarily need to have the project management experience for this exam but you will need some acquired certification prior to giving this exam.

This course is conducted by highly experienced faculty who has experience in project management and agile methodology.

Requirements for this course:

The candidate must hold a PRINCE2 Foundation certification or be a PMP credential holder in order to be a part of PINCE2 practitioner training. The lists of exams you clear to get this certification are:

PRINCE2 Foundation exam

  • IPMA Level- A (Certified Projects Director)
  • IPMA Level- B (Certified Senior Project Manager)
  • IPMA Level-C (Certified Project Manager)
  • Project Management Professional (PMP)
  • Certified Associate in Project Management (CAPM)
  • IPMA Level-D (Certified Project Management Associate)

Audience to target:

A project manager who wants to become PRINCE2 Practitioner.

Exam paper design:

  • The duration of the PRINCE2 practitioner exam is 150 minutes.
  • The total number of questions is 68.
  • You need at least 38 out of 68 to pass the exam which is 55%.
