About the Microsoft 70-740 Course

Microsoft 70-740 video course is all about the terms, concepts and the technology that is included in the MCSA certification. The course is designed for the professionals of the information technology whose work related to Windows Server. Due to the advancement of the technology, the course is recommended to all people who have any direct or indirect relationship with the IT. The candidates passing the course are responsible for the management of the storage and operate Windows Server.

Microsoft 70-740 Course Description

The prime focus of the course is the installation, computing functionality and features, managing storage in Windows Server. Generally, the topics covered by the course are installation, consideration, configuration of the Nano Server. It also includes the images management and creation for the deployment process. Further topics included in the course are the storage solutions, configuration of volumes and disks, increased availability, data duplication control, storage space, disaster recovery, and the failover solutions.

The candidates who want to pass the exam should have experience with the server and the local storage. He/she should be able to manage the containers and Hyper-V along with monitoring of servers in compute and physical environments.

Knowledge and skills students develop

After completing Microsoft 70-740 course, student have the following skills:

  • Preparation and installation of Nano server, the planning and migration strategy
  • Implementation and management of data deduplication and storage spaces
  • Installation and configuration of virtual machines and Microsoft Hyper-V
  • The concept of increased availability and risk recovery technologies in the Windows Server
  • Planning, creation, and management of failover cluster and deployment images
  • Maintenance, creation, and management of the installation of virtual machines
