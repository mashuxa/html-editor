200-105 Video Course description

Introduction:

200-105 is a video training that is intended to equip the candidates with the knowledge necessary to pass Cisco ICND2 exam. The candidates who pass this exam receive CCNA certification as the blueprint of their academic excellence. The training will not only equip the candidates with skills on networking concepts but also embraces the qualities of an ethical IT professional. This is the alternative to receive a certification from a recognized institution where your certificate will not be isolated because of the inferiority of the institution attended.

Description:

The course is offered at a standardized price to meet the requirements of the majority of professionals who are ambitious for this certification. The features of this training are unique in the sense that it focuses on the candidate’s achievement. Besides, the instructor, Matt Carey is proficient in teaching a networking course as seen in the reviews and performance of the students.

In this video course, you will learn several networking concepts starting with CCNA fundamentals, TCIP/OSI models, switching technologies, routing concepts, WAN, and packet tracer services. The clear understanding of these concepts is achieved by going through a series of video tutorial that addresses one unified concept at a time. The learning will afterward proceed to more advanced stuff such as deployment and troubleshooting of networking devices. However, the video lectures are explained using configuration examples, flowcharts, and diagrammatic representation of concepts.

Knowledge and skills students develop:

  • LAN Switching Techniques
  • Routing Technologies
  • WAN Setup Technologies
  • Network Infrastructure
  • OSI model
  • Communication Model
  • Packet Tracer Technology

Requirements for the course:

  • The candidate is needed to be familiar with networking terms before joining this course.
  • A desire to study by heart and be well conversant with networking acronyms is considered to be a core requirement.
  • The student taking this course should be ready to gather for personal items to be used during labs such as the simulators.
  • There should be keen attention to the key concepts, the learner should not take this course with unsettled mindset.

Exam paper design:

  • The exam is set to be done for 1 hour 30 minutes.
  • It is the exam of 65-75 questions from curriculum content.
  • The format adopted by 200-205 exam is multiple choice questions and answers.
  • The exam registration fee is $165 exclusive of the course registration fee.

Audience to target:

  • Undergraduate students who want to be the future network engineers are also the ideal candidates.
  • The students who failed any networking exam under the Cisco track are also considered part and parcel of this training.
